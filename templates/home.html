What is CoderDojo?

CoderDojo is a worldwide network of free programming clubs for young people aged 7–17. At each Dojo, young people learn to code, develop websites, build apps and games, and explore technology in a creative and collaborative environment

What will you do?

Our events and lessons are designed to be hands-on, engaging, and tailored to different skill levels. Here’s what you can expect when you join us:

  • Build real-world projects using Raspberry Pi, such as smart home devices or mini games.
  • Learn coding languages like Python, HTML and more through interactive challenges.
  • Experiment with hardware components like sensors, LEDs, and motors to bring ideas to life.
